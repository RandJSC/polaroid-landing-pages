3D Pens

Polaroid 3D printing technology made into a handheld device for colorful and easy-to-use 3D creativity. Bring all your favorite creations to life with your very own Polaroid 3D pen.

Polaroid 3D pens are the perfect tool for instantly designing beautiful works of art, creating objects or adding a little fun into learning.

3D pens are great for...

Crafting: Bridging the gap between technology and art, crafters can make a wide variety of projects from home décor to special keepsakes including keychains, buttons, and portraits.

Education: Polaroid 3D pens offer a unique, hands-on approach to subjects ranging from math and science to history and art. Whether you’re creating diaramas, geometric shapes, or biological cells, these pens bring school projects to life with fun and creativity.

Hobbies: Either with a stencil or drawing free-hand, use your 3D pen to design anything from model cars, model train set environments, plants, toys and more.

Jewelry: Home jewelry designers can bring wearable, colorful and completely customized pieces to life with the help of Polaroid 3D pens.

3D Printers

User-friendly Polaroid 3D printers enable you to create objects and art in your own home or office. Make your visions a reality with Polaroid 3D printers.

Ideal for at-home users, small businesses or classrooms, the line of Polaroid 3D printers make 3D printing technology easy- to-use and accessible to everyone.

3D printers are great for...

Architecture: Make beautiful, complex, and durable models for upcoming projects, helping clients see the home of their dreams.

Education: Well-suited for STEAM (Science, Technology, Engineering, Art, and Math) lessons in the classroom, 3D printers can offer visuals for complex concepts, promote problem solving skills and encourage creativity.

Home Decor: Add the finishing touches to any room design. With a Polaroid 3D printer, you can design and make your own décor such as lamps, vases, candle holders and more.

Hobbies: How do you make toys and games more fun? Design your own. Polaroid 3D printers make it possible to create toys and games pieces at home.
